The Challenge

Bridging the Gap Between Academia and Industry

Today's pharmacy graduates possess strong academic knowledge, yet many lack exposure to industry expectations, practical problem-solving, communication skills, innovation thinking, and professional confidence.

The healthcare industry is evolving rapidly with digital health, AI, automation, patient-centric care, and multidisciplinary collaboration becoming essential. Colleges need structured programs that prepare students for these changing demands.

"The OpenUp Healthcare Leadership Program bridges this exact gap."

About OpenUp

OpenUp is an experiential learning initiative that develops students beyond the classroom by combining innovation, design thinking, communication, leadership, AI, and industry exposure into one structured learning journey.

Unlike traditional workshops, OpenUp enables students to work on real healthcare challenges while building a professional portfolio and preparing for future careers.

Learning by Doing

Throughout the program, students work in teams to identify a real healthcare problem, conduct market research, develop innovative solutions, build prototypes, and present their work before an industry jury.

Every student graduates with practical experience—not just theoretical knowledge.
