I have personally conducted over 50 workshops and sessions on Design Thinking, Innovation, Entrepreneurship, Problem Solving, Career Development, and Future Skills across colleges, student organizations, and innovation ecosystems.

Additionally, I played a key role in the Practice School Program at Poona College of Pharmacy, where students underwent immersive industry exposure through startup visits, hospital visits, patient-centric healthcare observations, problem-solving projects, design thinking workshops, and final demonstration presentations.

Beyond academia, I also run FHBN (Firodia Hostel Business Network), an alumni-driven community that promotes peer learning, mentorship, collaboration, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial growth among students and professionals.

My long-term vision is to build systems and platforms that enable Indian youth to become globally competent, industry-ready, and capable of creating meaningful impact.
